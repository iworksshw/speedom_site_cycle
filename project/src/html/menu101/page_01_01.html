K-Cycle Racing (Korean Cycle Racing)

Official Olympic Event (KEIRIN): A cycling race where 7 riders compete for rankings by completing 5 laps on a 333.33 meter velodrome.

It has been stably operated as a betting sport in Korea for about 30 years, generating over $1.2 billion in annual revenue.

K-Motorboat Racing (Korean Motorboat Racing)

Motorboat racing, held on water, features 6 participants competing for rankings by completing 2 to 3 laps on a 600-meter racecourse.

Since 2002, it has been operating as a stable betting sport in Korea, generating annual revenue of over $ 470 million.
