Gwangmyeong Speedom

Cycle Stadium Speedome

Introducing the Gwangmyeong Speedom, an international-standard racetrack.
This indoor racetrack operates year-round and is constructed to meet the standards set by the International Cycling Union. It is a source of pride for South Korea, excelling in both scale and facilities on a global level.
Spectators can enjoy the races from general seating, specialised viewing areas, premium TV rooms, and membership lounges. The venue seamlessly combines leisure and recreation under an elegant interior design that enhances customer convenience.
Facilities include a third-floor cafeteria and a fourth-floor restaurant, as well as amenities such as children's playrooms, beginner classrooms, outdoor terraces, large-scale lounges, meeting spaces, and other rest areas—creating an environment where families can enjoy their visit together.

Location
Gwangmyeong Speedom, 721 Gwangmyeong-ro (Gwangmyeong-dong), Gwangmyeong-si, Gyeonggi-do, Republic of Korea
Area
Building Area: 39,670㎡
Total Floor Area: 76,033㎡
Track Specifications
Track Length: 333.3m
Track Width: 9.8m
Gradient: 4° on straights, 34.45° on curves
