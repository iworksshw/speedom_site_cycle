The overall prize money rankings of cycle racing athletes are offered only to those who have participated in at least 20% of the total race days up to the most recent round.

The prize money includes various allowances for standby and waiting days, and only results from Gwangmyeong (or Jamsil before 2006) are reflected.
The prize money rankings are based solely on results from races held in Gwangmyeong (excluding Changwon and Busan). These rankings apply to athletes who have participated in at least 20% of the total race days up to the most recent round. (For example: for the 20th race, athletes must have participated in at least 12 days (20%) out of a total of 60 days)
