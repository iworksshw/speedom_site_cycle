15th Race on Day 3 of the 27th Racing Series, 2024 (09 July)

  • 2024.07.05
  • Track Cycling Planning Team
  • Views: 2,000

■ Race No. 15
○ Involved Riders: No. 5 (Kim Se-jun), No. 6 (Kim Man-seop)
○ Applicable Regulations: Article 72, Paragraph 3 of the Racing Rules (Duty of Care in Racing), Article 74, Paragraph 2 (Prohibition of Obstructive Racing)
○ Judgement Details: Near the first corner during 4th lap, the lane change by rider No. 6 and improper racing by rider No. 5 combined, resulting in a complex situation causing rider No. 5 to fall. Based on Article 72, Paragraph 3, and Article 74, Paragraph 2, both riders (No. 5 and No. 6) were issued warnings.
